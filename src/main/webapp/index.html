About Us




A library is a place where there are lots of books. In libraries, one can read the book at that place or can borrow the book if the rules of the library permits. A library improves one's knowledge. One can read lot of books. A library contains books of various genres. There are stacks of books. The books will be organized on the basis of genre, author or age etc. A library has shelves of books neatly organized. There will be librarians who take care of the books there and keep the record. Every library maintain a record of their books and number the books. There is condition in all libraries. And, that is to keep silence. One is expected to be silent when in library. Thus, library has a peaceful atmosphere. Ancient libraries collected manuscripts. There are different types of libraries. Academic libraries are libraries within an educational institution. Children libraries are libraries that have books specially for children. There are national libraries that collect rare things. Public lending libraries lend books to the people. There also reference and research libraries. Thus, one can Learn a lot in a library. Don't miss your chance of going to library if you get a chance.
